Like to Game?

There are lots of games available online. Steam/Epic/Origin offer sales every season. With the holiday season right around the corner, savings in all shapes an sizes will be available!

Steam has a winter sale that starts in what's classified as the "Holiday Season" which is typically around December. You can get all your favorite franchise titles at a significant discount during these sales. The savings range anywhere from 10% to 90% off! Be sure to check Steam in December for all the greatest gaming goodies.

Holiday Season

There are a lot of fun activities available this time of year! Here's our top 3:

Ugly Christmas Sweater Contest: Host a friendly competition to see who can come up with the tackiest, most outrageous Christmas sweater. Bonus points for DIY embellishments! Holiday Movie Marathon: Create a cozy movie night with classic holiday films. Don't forget the popcorn and hot cocoa. You can even turn it into a movie marathon sleepover. Cookie Decorating Party: Bake a batch of sugar cookies and set up a decorating station with various icing colors, sprinkles, and edible glitter. It's a delicious and artsy activity.
